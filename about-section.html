Alvin Delve is a sports analyst, color commentator, and performance coach who has built a dynamic career at the intersection of sports broadcasting and biomechanics. He serves as a color analyst for ESPN, covering Division I competition across multiple sports, and delivers English-language analysis for global basketball broadcasts with FIBA Media.

At ESPN, Alvin has covered Division I events across the ASUN Conference, including March Madness tournament play and the ASUN Track & Field Championships. Internationally, he has served as the English-language analyst for Italy’s Serie A Championship broadcasts. His expertise in biomechanics and performance has also earned him appearances on FOX Sports as a fitness expert.

Alvin founded Delve Into Fitness, a biomechanics-driven coaching company that helps athletes and professionals improve movement efficiency, reduce injury risk, and maximize performance.

He stands out for his preparation, technical expertise, and ability to communicate complex concepts with clarity. Alvin consistently brings energy, insight, and authority to every broadcast, training session, and performance environment, establishing himself as a rising voice in sports.
