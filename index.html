Meet Alvin

Alvin Delve

Alvin Delve is an American sports analyst and color commentator calling collegiate athletics for ESPN’s platforms in the United States and international basketball for FIBA Media.

He is also the founder of Delve Into Fitness, a biomechanics-based performance company helping athletes improve movement, performance, and longevity.

A former NCAA Division I athlete, Alvin brings firsthand competitive experience to every broadcast, training session, and presentation.
