Meet Alvin

Built in competition. Trusted in the moment.

Alvin Delve is an ESPN analyst, football broadcaster, performance coach, speaker, and former NCAA Division I athlete. His work connects elite competition, clear storytelling, and biomechanics-based performance coaching.

Whether he is explaining a game, developing an athlete, or addressing an audience, Alvin makes complex ideas accessible, relevant, and actionable.

Read Full Bio →

As an ESPN+ analyst, Alvin has covered college basketball, volleyball, baseball, softball, football, and track & field across the ASUN Conference, including championship coverage for ASUN basketball and track & field.

His international broadcasting experience includes English-language championship basketball coverage in Italy’s Serie A. His expertise in fitness and performance has also led to appearances on FOX as a fitness expert.

Beyond the broadcast booth, Alvin founded Delve Into Fitness, a biomechanics and performance-based coaching company designed to help athletes and professionals move efficiently, reduce injury risk, and build sustainable performance.
